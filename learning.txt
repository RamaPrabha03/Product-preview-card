1. Why use margin: 0 and padding: 0?
	Browsers automatically add default margins and padding to elements like <body>, <h1>, and <p> tags so that unstyled websites are still readable.*, *::before, *::after {
  margin: 0;
  padding: 0;
  box-sizing: border-box;
}

The * (asterisk) is a universal selector, meaning it applies these rules to every single element on your page, including the "hidden" parts like pseudo-elements (::before and ::after).
